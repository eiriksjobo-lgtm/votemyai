Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2025

At VoteMyAI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use VoteMyAI, we collect:

  • Google Account Information: When you log in with Google, we receive your email address and name. We do not store your Google password.
  • User-Generated Content: Tracks you submit (YouTube links, titles, genre, AI tool used).
  • Voting Data: Records of which tracks you've voted on to prevent duplicate votes.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (page views, time on site, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the VoteMyAI platform
  • Display your username and submitted tracks publicly
  • Prevent voting fraud and abuse
  • Communicate important updates (rarely, if ever)
  • Analyze site performance and user behavior

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Supabase: Our authentication and database provider (secure, encrypted storage)
  • Google: For OAuth login (they already have your data)
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider
  • Google Analytics: For anonymized usage statistics

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all your submitted tracks in your profile
  • Delete your data: Delete individual tracks or your entire account
  • Request data export: Contact us for a copy of your data
  • Opt out of analytics: Use browser extensions like uBlock Origin

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication via Google OAuth, and Supabase's built-in security features. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication cookies: To keep you logged in
  • Voting prevention: Local browser storage to prevent duplicate votes
  • Google Analytics: To track site usage (can be blocked)

7. Children's Privacy

VoteMyAI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of VoteMyAI after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: contact@votemyai.com
